# Design-Parity Gap Inventory

Per-area record of deviations between the live app and the `project/` design source. Each entry: `aspect · detail · severity(High/Medium/Low) · status(open/closed/deferred)`. Definition of done: **0 open High/Medium**; Low may be `deferred` with reviewer sign-off (clarify Q2). Verified at review + token/structure assertions; no pixel-diff (clarify Q1). Built per area, interleaved (clarify Q3).
